# Executive Summary: AI/ML Salary Trends Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzed 6 years of salary data to forecast 2026-2027 compensation trends.

## Key Findings
- Average salary: $146,866
- Historical growth: 48% (2020-2023)
- Recent decline: 5% (2024-2025)
- Forecast: Moderate growth expected

## Methodology
- ARIMA time series modeling
- Segmentation by experience level
- 29,326 clean records analyzed

## Recommendations
1. Market stabilization expected
2. Senior roles remain valuable
3. Entry-level growth slowing
